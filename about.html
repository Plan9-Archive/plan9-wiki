About the Plan 9 wiki server

The pages marked ``(Plan 9 wiki)'' on this server are part of a wiki, a web space in which all pages are editable by anyone with a web browser or a Plan 9 installation. Thus the contents here should not be taken as statements by Alcatel-Lucent, Bell Labs, or anyone even remotely connected to them.

In order to keep the server free of objectionable material, all contents on this server are subject to change by the editor. We retain the right and announce the intention of turning off this wiki if it is abused. Please don't.
