Public Plan 9 Servers

A number of servers around the internet give out Plan 9 accounts. If
you'd like to play around with the system but haven't yet gotten a
cpu server of your own, this might be a good route for you.

Current:
 *	[9srv.net] Located in Ohio, United States.
 *	[Nein | http://rootne.in] For haters.

Historical:
 *	[tip9ug] - Tokyo Inferno/Plan 9 Users Group The longest running
	public system. Goes down sometimes. Located in Tokyo, Japan. For
	access, see http://www.tip9ug.jp/en/accounts.html
 *	[9grid.es | http://www.9grid.es] Located in Spain.
